# Maahefun Frisbee 2026

## Introduction

Kicking off Ramadan with a commemorative frisbee match and lots of food!

- The Theme of the commemorative match is Maahefun, to celebrate the arrival of Ramadan.
- It’s not a must to join the Maahefun if you are joining the match, but it is encouraged!
- It is going to be a potluck, so bring your favourite Maahefun food to share with the rest!

## Team Formation

Teams will be based on your ranking of the maahefun dishes. Either 2 or 4 teams will be formed based on the number of sign-ups. However, to balance fairness and enjoyment while maintaining the randomness of the draw, we will apply the following criteria:

1. Each team will have 7-12 players including substitutes. 
2. **A minimum of one handler per team.** If there are no handlers available in the pool, this criterion will be omitted.
3. **A minimum of one female player per team.** If there are not enough female players available in the pool, this criterion will be omitted.

## Games structure

- Each game will be played for a time limit of **20 minutes**.
- If the game is tied when the time cap is reached, it ends in a draw—except for the final match, where a universe point must be played to determine the winner.
- Matches will be played either 5v5, 6v6 or 7v7 depending on the total number of participants.
- UFA game rules will apply. For any disagreement that cannot be resolved internally by the players, which falls outside of what is covered in the UFA rule book, the ethics committee, together with the Exco will make the final decision.
- Each team gets unlimited substitutions. Substitutions can only be made before a pull, except for in cases of injuries or other unforeseen occurrences that the field leader deems to be a valid reason for substitution mid-game.
