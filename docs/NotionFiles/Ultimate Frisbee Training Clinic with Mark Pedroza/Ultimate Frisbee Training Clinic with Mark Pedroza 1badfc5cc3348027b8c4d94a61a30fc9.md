### Detailed Session Content

### Day 1: Friday, April 18

- **Morning: Skills, Drills, Throwing Analysis**
    - Fundamental throwing mechanics (backhand, forehand, hammer)
    - Cutting techniques and field awareness
    - Individual skill assessment and feedback
- **Evening: Advanced Strategy - Horizontal Stack**
    - Horizontal stack formation and spacing
    - Cutting patterns and timing
    - Defensive strategies against horizontal offenses
    - Practical application and situational drills

### Day 2: Saturday, April 19

- **Morning: Advanced Strategy - Zone Defense and Offense**
    - Cup formations and adjustments
    - Wing and deep defender positioning
    - Breaking zones effectively
    - Zone offense principles and execution
- **Evening: Advanced Strategy - Vertical Stack**
    - Vertical stack setup and maintenance
    - Creating and exploiting cutting lanes
    - Reset systems and bail options
    - Endzone offense from vertical sets
