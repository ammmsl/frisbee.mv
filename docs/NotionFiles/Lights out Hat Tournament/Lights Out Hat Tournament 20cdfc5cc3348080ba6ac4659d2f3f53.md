# Lights Out Hat Tournament

## Introduction

A Hat tournament is an Ultimate Frisbee format where players sign up individually rather than as teams. The name originates from the traditional method of placing players' names in a hat and drawing them randomly to form teams.

- The theme of our **June hat tournament** is glow-in-the-dark. Think neon, LED, and all things that light up the night!
- The tournament will follow a **league format**.
- Hat tournaments are all about **fun first**, with a dash of competitive spirit. Teams are mixed to keep it light, social, and inclusive.
- We’re looking for **volunteers** to help run the event smoothly; if you’re interested, please reach out!
- We use the hat tournaments as a way to collect player stats to help guide future events.

## Team Formation

Teams of will be formed at random, and we will adhere to this as much as possible. However, to balance fairness and enjoyment while maintaining the randomness of the draw, we will apply the following criteria:

1. Each team will have 7-9 players including substitutes. 
2. **A minimum of one handler per team.** If there are no handlers available in the pool, this criterion will be omitted.
3. **A minimum of one female player per team.** If there are not enough female players available in the pool, this criterion will be omitted.
4. The rest of the player names will be drawn out of a hat, the night of so please be on time!

Each win scores 3 points, while a draw earns 1 point. No points are deducted for a loss. 

If more than two teams are tied for the highest points, the tie will be broken using the following criteria, in order:

1. Highest goal difference
2. Highest number of goals scored
3. Least number of goals conceded
4. If still tied, the teams will play a universe point to determine who advances to the final.

## Games structure

- Each game will be played for a time limit of **20 minutes**.
- If the game is tied when the time cap is reached, it ends in a draw.
- Matches will be played either 5v5, 6v6 or 7v7 depending on the total number of participants.
- 4 teams will be formed, and the tournament will follow a league format.
- UFA game rules will apply. For any disagreement that cannot be resolved internally by the players, which falls outside of what is covered in the UFA rule book, the ethics committee, together with the Exco will make the final decision.
- Each team gets unlimited substitutions. Substitutions can only be made before a pull, except for in cases of injuries or other unforeseen occurrences that the field leader deems to be a valid reason for substitution mid-game.
- There will be a dedicated linesman at the endzones.

## Seeding Summary

- All hat tournaments will be used as an opportunity to seed and rank players.
- Players will get points for **participation, wins, goals, assists, blocks,** and demonstrating good **sportsmanship (spirit points).**
- The ranking will be used as a basis for all and any individual awards at the end of the year.
